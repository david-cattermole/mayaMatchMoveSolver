mmSolver.tools.screenzmanipulator¶

Tool¶

This tool aims the move manipulator tool at the active viewport camera. After aiming the move tool at the camera, the screen-space Z axis is highlighted, ready to be middle-click dragged.

mmSolver.tools.screenzmanipulator.tool.screen_space_z(camera_tfm)¶

Modifies moveManipContext to custom and points one of the axis to the camera.

Parameters:camera_tfm (str) – Camera to point to
Returns:None
mmSolver.tools.screenzmanipulator.tool.main()¶
